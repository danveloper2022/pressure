Our Services

We use only the best equipment to ensure a top quality job, from start to finish. We are fully insured and WorkSafe BC covered. Ask us about maintenance schedules!

POWER WASHING

Driveways, walkways, pool patios, stairways, fences, concrete, custom houses, small strata buildings and businesses.

WINDOW CLEANING

Exterior windows and skylights. We take great care to ensure a top quality finish when it comes to cleaning your windows. We use only the best products with purified water fed poles as well as the traditional squeegee method to give you a view worth bragging about.

GUTTER CLEANING

We keep your gutters clear of debris to help prevent leaks and flooding and maintain the value of your home.

ROOF TREATMENT

Low pressure solution to treating moss and algae on your roof. Having moss on your roof can rot your roof and mean a new roof well before the lifespan is up. Keep your roof sparkling clean and free of mildew for a better look and value.

HOME SERVICES

Contact us for other home maintenance services and ask us about creating a maintenance schedule for your property. Knowing your investment will be serviced when you need it gives you peace of mind.
